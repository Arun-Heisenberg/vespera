Objective: Build a world-class, production-ready e-commerce flagship for "Vespera"—an elite boutique for curated evening minaudières.

1. Brand Identity & Asset Integration:

Brand Name: Vespera.

Logo: Use the uploaded logo.png in the navigation bar and footer. Ensure it is rendered with high fidelity and proper scaling.

Aesthetic: "High-Fashion Editorial." Minimalist, high whitespace, cinematic transitions.

Palette: Obsidian (#0A0A0A) background, Pure Alabaster (#FFFFFF) text, and Brushed Champagne (#D4AF37) for accents/buttons.

Payments: Stripe SDK for secure, frictionless checkout.

3. Database Schema (Supabase):
Initialize tables for:

collection: (id, title, description, price, stock_count, primary_image, details_json).

orders: (id, status, total_amount, stripe_session_id, shipping_details).

customers: (id, email, full_name).
